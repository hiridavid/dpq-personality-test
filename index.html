Welcome!

The DPQ Personality Test (test) is an advanced behaviour test developed by Jones, A. C. and in collaboration with Amanda Jones in 2009 as a Ph.D. Thesis in the University of Texas, Austin. This online form (form), based on the test, was developed by Hiri Dávid in 2023 in Örbottyán, Hungary. The form is a non-commercial modification of the test and can not accurately replace the original, only display a close approximation on a more user-friendly interface.

Please adjust the slider on each card to accurately represent how strongly you agree or disagree with the statement above regarding the subject of your form (yourself, or another person of choice).

Your results are calculated automatically. We do not store any user data. Please screenshot your results (mobile) or download the webpage (Ctrl+S on PC) in order to save & share them.
